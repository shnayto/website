Harmony of the Spheres is a VR application which allows you to create a musical solar system. Available for the Meta Quest 2 here.




'What's Alan?' is an electroacoustic composition that uses the voice of Alan Watts to explore the quirks and oddities of AI voice cloning technology.





'By the Sea' is a song to soothe your ears if you've just listened to 'What's Alan?'





Casioclone is a sample pack based on the Casiotone MT-65 from 1983. Available on Pianobook here.




'Bernard Moulds His Bread Into Pellets and Calls Them People' is a spatial composition. I've cherry picked the most absurd lines from each of the six characters in Virginia Woolf's 'The Waves', and created this whirlwind of an audio experience around them. Listen to the binaural mix with headphones below.





The Spaceman Who Came To Tea is a short film by Scott O'Keefe. I worked as the sound designer and composer




Biddy's is a virtual environment based on the area of Feakle where Biddy Early once lived.
